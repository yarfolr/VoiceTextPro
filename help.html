Help — How this site works

This demo is a minimal client-side speech-to-text playground built for demonstration and local use.

Important notes & limitations

  • The site uses your browser's built-in SpeechRecognition (Web Speech API). Not all browsers support it. Chrome (desktop) and some Chromium-based browsers have better support.
  • Transcription quality depends on the browser engine, microphone quality, and selected language.
  • Uploading audio files does not automatically produce perfect transcription. The "Transcribe while playing" option will play your uploaded audio and use the microphone to capture it — this relies on your system capturing speaker output via microphone, which is not ideal. For accurate audio-file transcription you would normally use a server-side model (e.g., Whisper) or cloud Speech-to-Text APIs.
  • When recording live (Record), the app requests microphone permission. Allow it. The live transcription appears as you speak. Use headphones or quiet environment for best results.
  • After recording you can download the transcript as .txt or copy to clipboard.

How to use

  1. Click "Let's start" on the landing page.
  2. Choose your language from the grid. The app will use that language for recognition.
  3. Either paste text, upload a .txt or audio file, or record using your microphone.
  4. If you upload audio, press "Play uploaded" to listen, or "Transcribe while playing" to try to capture it via your microphone (requires mic permission).
  5. To record live: press Record → Start recording, speak, then Stop. The transcribed text will appear live.

New Features

  • History: Your transcriptions are automatically saved and can be accessed through the History button in the menu. The last 10 transcriptions are stored locally in your browser.
  • Settings: Customize your experience through the Settings menu:
    • Theme selection (dark mode, with light mode coming soon)
    • Toggle automatic saving of transcriptions
  • Enhanced Navigation: The new menu provides quick access to History, Settings, and Help, with intuitive icons for better usability.

Privacy

This is a local client-side demo and does not send your audio or text to any server. However, speech recognition engines are implemented by the browser vendor and may send audio to their services depending on implementation. Check your browser's privacy policy if this is a concern.
